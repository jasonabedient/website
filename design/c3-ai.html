About C3.ai

C3 AI (NYSE: AI) is an enterprise AI application software company offering integrated products including the C3 Agentic AI Platform, C3 AI Applications (industry-specific SaaS), and C3 Generative AI. For more information, visit c3.ai.

My Role

As Design Director at C3.ai, I lead enterprise AI product design from strategy through delivery. I focus on turning complex, high-stakes workflows into intuitive experiences—while building the systems (design standards, tokens, and design-to-code practices) that help teams ship consistent, scalable enterprise products.

Key Responsibilities

  • Lead design strategy and end-to-end product delivery for enterprise AI platforms and applications.
  • Partner with product and engineering to simplify complex workflows into intuitive user experiences.
  • Drive design quality and consistency through a scalable design system and token infrastructure.
  • Establish modern design-to-code workflows to improve speed, alignment, and enterprise-grade execution.

My work at C3 AI is at the intersection of complex data visualization and next-generation agentic AI, ensuring that enterprise users can harness the power of AI through clear and effective interfaces.
