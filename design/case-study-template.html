Fintech Dashboard Redesign

Simplifying complex financial workflows for enterprise users to increase productivity and reduce errors.

Role

Lead Product Designer

Timeline

3 Months (Q2 2025)

Team

1 PM, 2 Fe Developers

Tools

Figma, Principle

The Challenge

The legacy dashboard was cluttered, slow, and difficult to navigate. Users reported spending too much time searching for critical data, leading to a 15% drop in efficiency.

The Approach

We started with user interviews to understand the core tasks. The goal was to declutter the interface and surface high-priority information immediately.

Key Insights

  • Users only use 20% of the features 80% of the time.
  • Data visualization was confusing and misaligned with user expectations.
  • Navigation depth was too high (3+ clicks to reach key pages).

Wireframes exploring different navigation patterns.

The Solution

We implemented a modular dashboard allowing users to customize their view. We simplified the navigation into a primary sidebar and context-aware secondary actions.

Outcome

Post-launch metrics showed a 25% increase in task completion speed and a significant reduction in support tickets related to navigation.
