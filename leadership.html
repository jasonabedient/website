Leading Teams Through the AI Transition

Leading a design org inside an enterprise AI company requires a fundamental shift in mindset. We are no longer just designing static interfaces; we are designing collaborative intelligence. I focus on upskilling teams to work alongside AI—using it for rapid prototyping and discovery—while maintaining the human-centered rigor necessary for trust and safety in enterprise software.

Leadership Philosophy

My leadership style is rooted in empathy, transparency, and a relentless focus on user value. I believe that high-performing teams are built on trust and psychological safety, where every member feels empowered to share ideas and challenge the status quo.

I strive to create an environment where designers can do their best work, untethered by bureaucracy but aligned with strategic business goals.

I find inspiration from my teammates and colleagues just as much as from creative tasks. I've managed teams for many years in almost every job I've had. Developing my skills and experience has boosted my confidence in working with diverse people and supporting their careers.

Team Building

Building a team is more than just hiring skills; it's about curating a diverse mix of perspectives and backgrounds. I focus on hiring for potential and cultural add, not just cultural fit.

I advocate for cross-functional collaboration, ensuring that design, product, and engineering work as partners from discovery to delivery.

Mentorship

Mentorship is a two-way street. I am passionate about helping designers grow their careers, whether they want to deepen their craft or explore management paths.

I believe in regular 1:1s that focus on personal growth, not just status updates. I encourage my team to set ambitious goals and provide the support they need to achieve them.
